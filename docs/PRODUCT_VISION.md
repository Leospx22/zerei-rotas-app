# Zerei Rotas Product Vision

## Mission

Help last-mile delivery drivers turn a spreadsheet into a clear, reliable route they can organize, execute, and complete without losing time or delivery data.

## Vision

Zerei Rotas will become the daily operating companion for independent delivery drivers in Brazil: simple enough to start in minutes, dependable throughout the route, and intelligent enough to improve each working day.

The product should reduce administrative effort before a route, cognitive load during delivery, and uncertainty after completion.

## Target Customer

The primary customer is an independent or partner last-mile driver who receives delivery manifests as XLSX, XLS, or CSV files, initially focused on Shopee delivery workflows in Brazil.

Typical characteristics:

- Uses an Android phone as the primary work device.
- Handles many packages and stops under time pressure.
- Receives route data in spreadsheets rather than a driver-friendly execution tool.
- Needs the application to work reliably during long shifts and inconsistent connectivity.
- Values speed, clarity, and data safety more than complex fleet-management features.

Secondary future customers may include drivers working with other marketplaces and small delivery teams that share similar spreadsheet-based workflows.

## Customer Pain Points

- Spreadsheets are difficult to use safely while driving and delivering.
- Package, stop, and address relationships are hard to understand at a glance.
- Duplicate or inconsistent addresses create avoidable confusion.
- Drivers can lose progress when switching applications, selecting files, or restarting the phone.
- It is difficult to track delivered packages, skipped deliveries, and remaining work accurately.
- Generic route tools do not understand Shopee-oriented spreadsheet columns and stop grouping.
- Drivers have little useful history about duration, distance, difficult stops, or productivity.
- Existing tools may be too expensive, too complex, or designed for fleet managers rather than individual drivers.

## Value Proposition

Zerei Rotas converts a delivery spreadsheet into an actionable mobile route in a few steps:

1. Import the file.
2. Confirm packages and stops.
3. Organize and prepare the route.
4. Track deliveries and occurrences.
5. Complete the route with a reliable history record.

The core promise is: **from spreadsheet to completed route, without losing control of the operation.**

## Competitive Advantages

- Built around the real spreadsheet workflow of Brazilian delivery drivers.
- Native understanding of packages, Stop values, address subgroups, and duplicate-address warnings.
- Planning routes appear immediately and survive tab changes and application restarts.
- A single route state model keeps Painel, Minhas Rotas, and Historico consistent.
- Simple, driver-first interface instead of fleet-management complexity.
- Offline-first route persistence for the critical execution workflow.
- Low planned premium price relative to the time saved per route.
- Future AI and voice assistance designed around explicit driver confirmation and deterministic route data.

## Pricing Strategy

The target Premium Driver price is **R$9.90 per month**.

Pricing principles:

- Keep the essential import-to-completion workflow accessible enough to prove value.
- Charge for durable productivity benefits such as advanced statistics, backup and restore, favorites, templates, smart grouping, and insights.
- Make the monthly value easy to recover through time saved on a small number of routes.
- Avoid hiding data recovery or basic route reliability behind a paywall.
- Validate willingness to pay with active drivers before introducing annual or team plans.
- Consider a free trial or clearly limited free tier when premium capabilities are production-ready.

AI capabilities may become part of a higher-value plan only after usage cost, reliability, privacy, and measurable driver benefit are understood.

## Success Metrics

### Activation

- Percentage of new users who successfully import a first spreadsheet.
- Percentage of successful imports that become planning routes.
- Time from application open to first valid route preview.

### Core Reliability

- Successful import rate by file type and platform.
- Route completion rate without data loss or restart failure.
- Persistence failure rate for current route and history.
- Crash-free route sessions.
- Percentage of completed routes producing exactly one correct history entry.

### Engagement

- Weekly active drivers.
- Routes imported and completed per active driver.
- Driver retention after 7, 30, and 90 days.
- Percentage of drivers returning to view or reuse route history.

### Premium

- Trial-to-paid conversion.
- Monthly paid retention and churn.
- Usage of statistics, backup, favorites, templates, and smart grouping.
- Reported or estimated time saved per route.

### Quality

- Support requests per 100 completed routes.
- Import correction rate and duplicate-route rate.
- Automated regression coverage for confirmed bugs.
- Android and iOS release-checklist pass rate.

## Future Vision

Zerei Rotas should evolve in three stages:

1. **Reliable MVP:** make spreadsheet import, route preparation, execution, completion, and recovery dependable.
2. **Premium Driver:** add statistics, favorites, backup, templates, smart grouping, and productivity insights worth R$9.90/month.
3. **AI Driver Assistant:** analyze routes, identify risks, explain recommendations, and provide safe voice interaction without replacing deterministic route logic.

Long term, the product can support additional marketplaces and delivery formats while remaining focused on the individual driver's daily experience. Expansion must preserve the product's defining traits: fast setup, operational clarity, trustworthy persistence, and driver control.
